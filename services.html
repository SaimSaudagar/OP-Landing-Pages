Scan & Stay

Scan & Stay QR Visitor/Customer Systems

OnlyPark's proprietary Scan & Stay solution is a game-changer in customer and visitor parking management. Designed in-house and exclusive to OnlyPark, this QR-based system enables fast, seamless parking registration through a branded, mobile-friendly interface.

Customers simply scan a displayed QR code on arrival, select their destination (retailer, tenancy, etc.), and enter their registration details. Our system instantly grants them a pre-configured duration of stay‚Äîno physical tickets, no staff involvement.

All activity is visible through the OnlyPark Dashboard, which allows property managers to monitor usage, manage site rules and automate parking permissions. Whether you're managing a shopping centre, hotel or commercial building, Scan & Stay delivers a smarter, safer way to handle short-term parking.
